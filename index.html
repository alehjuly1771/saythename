SEVENTEEN adalah BoyBand besar dibawah naungan Pledis Entertaiment. Dengan 13 anggota, 3 sub-unit yaitu Vocal, HipHop, dan Performances. Kemudian menerapkan sistem pre-debut, SEVENTEEN's pelatihan dan persiapan berhasil diperkenalkan ke publik dengan cara live broadcasts.


Memulai Debut pada 26 Mei 2015 dengan Mini Album 17 CARAT. Suksesnya debut, mereka meninggalkan kesan kuat sebagai grup baru yang berbakat. Sejauh itu pula, mereka mencapai kesuksesan yang mendunia, mempengaruhi lewat promosi, penampilan, musik yang menjadi ciri khas, juga dikenal luas sebagai grup yang memproduksi lagu secara mandiri.

ALBUM

SEVENTEEN 5th Album 'HAPPY BURSTDAY'

The Album includes sixteen songs, including its lead single, "THUNDER", and a solo song from each of the members of SEVENTEEN. Among the songs are sollaboration with American record producers Pharrel Williams and Timbalad.

SEVENTEEN 10th Mini Album 'FML'

Prior to release, FML became the second most-preordered album in South Korean history, with 4.64 million preorders. Pledis and album distributor YG Plus, combined sales of all versions of FML surpassed 6.2 million copies in July 2023, marking a first for an album by a K-pop artist. It won Album of the Year at the 2023 MAMA Awards.

SEVENTEEN 11th Mini Album 'SEVENTEENTH HEAVEN'

Produced by Woozi and Bumzu, the eight-track EP is a primally upbeat record, serving as a celebration of the band's career thus far. It is led by the single "GOD OF MUSIC", and includes a collaboration with the American producer Marshmello title "SOS"

ALWAYS YOURS

The album features all the group's Japanesesongs from 2018-2023, alongside two new tracks, which include the lead single "IMA-EVEN IF THE WORLD ENDS TOMORROW". Upon release, the album charted at number one on Japan's Oricon and Billboard weekly charts, as well as at number five on both of their year-end charts.

SEVENTEEN 4th Album Repackage 'SECTOR 17'

It was released on October 14, 2024, by Pledis Entertainment through YG Plus. The EP's lead single, "Love, Money, Fame", was released on the same day and features American music producer DJ Khaled. Both the album and its lead single topped South Korea's respective Circle Charts.

SEVENTEEN 4th Album 'Face the Sun

The album contains nine tracks, with "Hot" serving as lead single. The first track, "Darl+ing", was pre-released on April 15, 2022. The repackaged version of the album, titled Sector 17, released on July 18 alongside its lead single "World". The fourth track, "Cheers", was pre-released on July 7, 2022.

Attaca: 9th Mini Album

Attacca is the tenth Korean extended play (EP) and twelfth overall by the South Korean boy band Seventeen released on October 22, 2021 by Pledis Entertainment. The EP contains seven tracks, which include the lead single "Rock with You".

An Ode

The album spawned two singles, "Hit" was released as the album's lead single on August 5, 2019, followed by the title track, "Fear", on the release date of the album. An Ode was a commercial success, debuted atop the South Korean Gaon Album Chart, and topped the Japanese Oricon Albums Chart in its third week. It also reached number seven on the US Billboard World Albums chart. An Ode sold 822,265 copies in South Korea in September 2019.

TEEN, AGE

It was released on November 6, 2017 by Pledis Entertainment with the lead single "Clap". Teen, Age was the group's second number one on the Billboard World Album Charts. The album was later repackaged as Seventeen's first special album entitled Director's Cut on February 5, 2018, with the title track "Thanks".

Going Seventeen

Going Seventeen is the third extended play from South Korean boy band Seventeen. It was released on December 5, 2016, by Pledis Entertainment. The album consists of eight tracks, including the title track, "Boom Boom".

Love & Letter (Repackage)

Love & Letter, also known as First 'Love & Letter', is the debut studio album by South Korean boy group Seventeen. It was released on April 25, 2016 through Pledis Entertainment. The album is a follow-up to the group's first two EPs, 17 Carat and Boys Be.
